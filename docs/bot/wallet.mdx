# Wallet Management

DeepMirror uses **zkLogin** to create a self-custodial Sui wallet tied to your Google account. No seed phrases, no private keys to manage‚Äîjust sign in and trade.

## Understanding zkLogin

zkLogin is Sui's innovative authentication system that combines:

- **OAuth 2.0** - Familiar Google sign-in flow
- **Zero-knowledge proofs** - Cryptographic security without exposing credentials
- **Blockchain addresses** - Deterministic wallet generation from your Google account

### How It Works

1. You sign in with Google
2. zkLogin generates a zero-knowledge proof of your OAuth token
3. A unique Sui address is derived from your Google account ID
4. The bot stores your ephemeral key pair (valid for 7 days)
5. Transactions are signed using the ephemeral key + ZK proof

<Info>
  Your wallet address is **deterministic** - signing in with the same Google
  account always gives you the same address.
</Info>

The wallet screen shows:

- **DEEP Balance** - DEEP tokens for trading fees
- **zkLogin Status** - Authentication state (Active/Expired)

## Sign In with zkLogin

### First-Time Setup

1. Tap **üîê Wallet** from main menu
2. Select **Sign in with Google**
3. Click the authentication link sent by the bot
4. Complete Google OAuth in your browser:
   - Choose your Google account
   - Grant permissions
   - Copy the redirect URL
5. Paste the redirect URL back in Telegram
6. Your wallet is created!

<Tip>
  The OAuth flow opens in your default browser. Make sure you have access to
  copy the final redirect URL!
</Tip>

### Session Management

zkLogin sessions expire after **7 days** for security. When expired:

- You'll see "‚ùå zkLogin not active" in wallet screen
- Simply sign in again with the same Google account
- Your wallet address remains unchanged
- All positions and settings persist

Balances update automatically when you open the wallet screen. To manually refresh:

1. Go to **üîê Wallet**
2. Tap **‚Üª Refresh Balance**
3. Wait 2-3 seconds for on-chain query

## Export Your Wallet (Advanced)

Want full control? You can export your wallet to use in other Sui wallets like Sui Wallet or Suiet.

<Warning>
  Exporting your private key is an **advanced feature**. Store it securely -
  anyone with this key controls your funds!
</Warning>

### How to Export

1. Go to **üîê Wallet**
2. Tap **üîë Export Wallet**
3. Confirm the security warning
4. Receive your wallet details:
   - **Address** - Your public Sui address
   - **Private Key** - Your secret key (keep safe!)
   - **Google Account** - Linked OAuth account

5. Import into any Sui wallet using the private key

### After Exporting

You can now:

- Use your wallet in Sui Wallet browser extension
- Access it via command-line tools
- Backup your private key in a password manager
- Continue using zkLogin in the bot (works independently)

<Note>
  Even after exporting, zkLogin continues to work. The bot maintains its own
  ephemeral keys separate from your exported private key.
</Note>

## Security Best Practices

### DO ‚úÖ

- Keep your Google account secure with 2FA
- Monitor your balance regularly
- Sign out when not actively trading
- Export and backup your wallet if holding significant value
- Verify transaction details before confirming

### DON'T ‚ùå

- Share your zkLogin redirect URLs with anyone
- Use a shared Google account
- Store large amounts on testnet (it's for testing!)
- Ignore session expiration warnings
- Trust unsolicited messages asking for wallet access

## Gas and Fee Management

### Gas Fees (SUI)

Every blockchain transaction requires gas:

| Operation         | Typical Cost  |
| ----------------- | ------------- |
| Placing an order  | 0.01-0.03 SUI |
| Creating position | 0.02-0.05 SUI |
| Canceling order   | 0.01-0.02 SUI |
| Capability grant  | 0.03-0.05 SUI |

<Tip>
  Keep at least **2 SUI** in your wallet for smooth operation. The bot will warn
  you when balance is low.
</Tip>

### Trading Fees (DEEP)

DeepBook V3 charges fees for order placement:

- With DEEP: **0.02%** (discounted)
- With base tokens: **0.03%** (standard)

DeepMirror **always uses DEEP** for lower fees.

Example:

- Position size: 10 SUI
- Fee: 10 √ó 0.0002 = 0.002 SUI worth of DEEP
- Approximately: 0.02-0.05 DEEP tokens
