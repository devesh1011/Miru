# Quickstart Guide

Get your DeepMirror bot up and running in three simple steps.

## Step 1: Launch the Bot

Open the DeepMirror bot in Telegram:

<Card title="Launch DeepMirror Bot" icon="telegram" href="https://t.me/deepmirror_bot">
  Open @deepmirror_bot in Telegram
</Card>

Tap `/start` to initialize your account.

## Step 2: Authenticate with zkLogin

DeepMirror uses Sui's zkLogin for secure, passwordless authentication:

1. Tap **üîê Wallet** from the main menu
2. Select **Sign in with Google**
3. Complete the OAuth flow in your browser
4. Return to Telegram - your wallet is ready!

<Note>
  zkLogin creates a Sui wallet tied to your Google account. No seed phrases to remember!
</Note>

Your wallet address will be displayed in the chat. Save it for depositing funds.

## Step 3: Fund Your Wallet

Send testnet tokens to your wallet address:

- **SUI** - For gas fees (minimum 0.5 SUI recommended)
- **DEEP** - For DeepBook trading fees (minimum 10 DEEP)

## Step 4: Add a Maker to Copy

Now you're ready to set up copy trading!

### Find a Maker Wallet

Choose a wallet address you want to copy. You can:
- Use a known profitable trader's address
- Find makers on DeepBook V3 analytics
- Test with your own maker wallet

### Configure Copy Trading

1. Tap **üìä Copy Trading** from the main menu
2. Select **Add Maker**
3. Enter the maker's wallet address
4. Choose your position sizing strategy:
   - **Fixed ratio** - Copy at a specific percentage (e.g., 10%)
   - **Fixed amount** - Use same SUI amount every time
   - **Match maker** - Copy their exact position size

5. Tap **Activate** to start monitoring

<Tip>
  Start with a **10% ratio** for your first position to limit risk while you learn.
</Tip>

## Step 5: Monitor Your Positions

Once active, the bot monitors your maker 24/7:

1. When the maker places an order on DeepBook V3, you receive a notification
2. Your copy position is automatically created at your configured ratio
3. You get a confirmation message with position details

View all positions anytime with `/positions` command.

## What Happens Next?

Your bot is now actively monitoring! Here's what to expect:

### When Maker Places an Order

1. **Detection** - Bot detects maker's new position within milliseconds
2. **Calculation** - Computes your position size based on your ratio
3. **Execution** - Places your order on DeepBook V3
4. **Notification** - Sends you a Telegram message with details
5. **Tracking** - Monitors position status and order count

### Position Lifecycle

Your copy positions will:
- **Monitor** - Track maker's active orders
- **Execute** - Place orders when maker does
- **Update** - Sync order counts in real-time
- **Notify** - Alert you of any changes

## Example Workflow

Here's a complete example:

```
1. Maker opens position: SUI/DBUSDC pool
2. You receive notification: "üéØ New maker position detected!"
3. Your ratio: 10%

```

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Wallet not created after zkLogin">
    Make sure you completed the OAuth flow in your browser. If stuck, tap **Sign in with Google** again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Position not copying">
    Check:
    - Do you have enough SUI for gas?
    - Do you have DEEP tokens for trading fees?
    - Is your copy trading activated? (check with `/copy`)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="zkLogin session expired">
    Sessions expire after 7 days. Simply sign in with Google again - your wallet remains the same!
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Transaction failed">
    Most common causes:
    - Insufficient SUI for gas fees
    - Insufficient DEEP for trading fees
    - Pool doesn't exist or is inactive
    - Check transaction hash on Sui Explorer for details
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
